About WatermelonGhost

Our Story

WatermelonGhost was born from a passion for blending nostalgic web aesthetics with cutting-edge technology. We believe in the beauty of the early internet - a time of experimentation, personal expression, and genuine connection.

While we embrace the retro visual language of Web 1.0, our technological approach is thoroughly modern. We're here to help businesses navigate digital transformation with solutions that are both innovative and human-centered.

We're not just another digital agency - we're digital time travelers.

Our Philosophy

We believe that digital experiences should be:

  • Authentic - True to your brand and values
  • Accessible - Available to all users regardless of ability
  • Efficient - Fast-loading and resource-conscious
  • Human - Built for people, not algorithms
  • Playful - Delivering joy and delight
